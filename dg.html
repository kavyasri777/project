Biodegradable

Biodegradable wastes are such waste materials which are and can be degraded by natural factors like microbes , and abiotic elements like temperature, UV, oxygen, etc. Some examples of such wastes are food materials, kitchen wastes.

Non-degradable substances are materials that cannot be broken down by natural organisms and remain in the environment for thousands of years. They are a source of pollution and can harm human life and other organisms.
