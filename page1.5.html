Palm tree furniture is made from the trunks and byproducts of palm trees, such as midribs, and can be a sustainable alternative to traditional timber. Here are some things to know about palm tree furniture: HistoryPalm wood has been used in furniture design for almost 100 years, becoming popular during the Art Deco period..

palm trees can be utilized as a material for furniture production. Research has explored the potential of various palm tree species, such as oil palms, coconut palms, and sugar palm trees, for manufacturing furniture products[1][2][3]. While traditional timber sources are depleting, palm trees offer a sustainable alternative due to their abundance and rapid growth. Studies have shown that palm tree byproducts, like midribs and trunks, possess suitable mechanical properties for furniture applications[3][4][5]. Innovative approaches have been developed to process palm tree waste into furniture components, including tables, chairs, and wardrobes, demonstrating the feasibility of using palm trees as a viable material source for the furniture industry
